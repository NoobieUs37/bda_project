The Spastics Society of Tamil Nadu (SPASIN)

A Centre of Excellence for People with Disabilities

The Spastics Society of Tamil Nadu (SPASIN) is a Community Based Organisation that endeavors to be a Centre of Excellence for people with disabilities. This organization works with self-help groups, following the human rights model of disability, and providing legal aid services for people with disabilities. Our project aims to showcase the impact of SPASTN's work on individuals and the community and will highlight the organization's mission and services. Additionally, it will provide some services based on Computer Technology for individuals with disabilities and their families. The project will be a valuable resource for individuals seeking assistance or wanting to support the organization.
